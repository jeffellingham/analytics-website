Certificates and Courses

Google's Data Analytics Professional Certificate: 180 hours across 8 courses covering every phase and tool of the data analytics process from asking, preparing, and processing/cleaning the data to analyzing, sharing/visualizing, and acting on it. Along the way I learned how analysts use tools like Excel, Sheets, R, Tableau, and SQL, not to mention every other imaginable bit of job-relevant knowledge needed to start and succeed as a Data Analyst.

Data Visualization with Tableau Specialization: 60 hours across 5 courses, this specialization by UC Davis taught the fundamentals of using Tableau as a visualization tool, as well as design principles, visual analytics, creating dashboards and story point presentations, and a capstone project.

SQL for Data Science: Taught by UC Davis.

Microsoft Excel - Excel from Beginner to Advanced : 21 hours of video and exercises covering everything from the basics to PivotTables, lookup functions (VLOOKUP and INDEX/MATCH), PowerPivot, charts, and the last third covered VBA.

Google Analytics Certification: Completed the Google course, and a supplemental one on Udemy, to become proficient with Google Analytics and receive the certification.
